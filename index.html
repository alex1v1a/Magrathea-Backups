About Us

Why Paper Trail?

We believe great software tells a story.

📖

Our Story

Paper Trail Limited was founded in the heart of Auckland, New Zealand, born from a simple belief: great software should tell a compelling story. Our journey began when our founder, frustrated by apps that promised much but delivered little, decided to create something different. Starting from a small office in Parnell, we set out to build mobile experiences that genuinely matter.

Each app we create represents a new chapter in our ongoing narrative of innovation. We don't just write code; we craft digital experiences that solve real problems for real people. Our New Zealand roots give us a unique perspective—we're used to doing more with less, to finding creative solutions in a place known as the edge of the world. This mindset shapes everything we build.

From our first prototype to our latest release, we've stayed true to our founding principle: technology should serve people, not the other way around. Every line of code, every pixel, every user interaction is an opportunity to tell that story.

🎯

Our Mission

Our mission is straightforward yet ambitious: build apps that solve real problems with honesty and elegance. We reject the culture of feature bloat and dark patterns. Instead, we focus on what matters—creating intuitive, reliable software that respects users and their time.

We believe in transparency, quality craftsmanship, and the power of negative space. No fluff, just function. This philosophy guides every decision we make, from initial concept to final release. We're not interested in building apps that harvest data or manipulate behavior. We build tools that empower users and make their lives genuinely better.

As a New Zealand-based company, we bring a unique perspective to the global app market. We understand that great ideas can come from anywhere, and we're committed to proving that world-class software can be built from the edge of the world.
