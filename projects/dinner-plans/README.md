# Dinner Plans Automation System

## Overview
Automated weekly meal planning system for family of 4 (2 adults, 2 children)

## Configuration
- **Budget:** $200/week
- **Store:** H-E-B (HEB.com)
- **Portions:** 2 adults + 2 children
- **Meals per week:** 7 unique dinners (no repeats from 100-night history)
- **Ingredient buffer:** 110% of required amounts
- **Dinner time:** 5:00 PM
- **Planning day:** Sundays at 9:00 AM

## Requirements
- Healthy, balanced meals (vegetables, proteins, organic when possible)
- No high fructose corn syrup
- No harmful chemicals/additives
- Include healthy fruits and snacks
- Different meal each night (tracked against 100-night history)
