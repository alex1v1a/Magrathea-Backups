--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. EMAIL CAPTURE STRATEGY OVERHAUL
   - Overall 5.9% email capture rate is critically low for outreach campaigns
   - Only 2 regions (Wisconsin, Connecticut) achieved >50% email capture
   - Recommendation: Implement secondary email discovery sources or manual
     verification for high-value regions

2. PRIORITIZE HIGH-PERFORMING REGIONS
   - Wisconsin and Connecticut demonstrate that quality email data IS available
   - Focus future operations on similar regional profiles
   - Analyze what made these regions successful (data source quality, etc.)

3. PHONE DATA IS MORE RELIABLE
   - 15% of shops have phone numbers vs 6% with emails
   - Consider phone-based outreach as primary channel for this dataset
   - Phone numbers collected: 96 total

4. WEBSITE DATA GAPS
   - Only 20% of shops have recorded websites
   - Missing website data limits ability to manually find contact info
   - Future operations should prioritize website discovery

5. REGIONAL EXPANSION OPPORTUNITIES
   - Large markets like California (23 shops, 0 emails) represent untapped potential
   - Consider manual enrichment for major manufacturing hubs
   - Texas performed well (28% email rate) - replicate approach in similar states

6. DEDUPLICATION EFFECTIVENESS

   - Low cross-region duplication validates the multi-region approach
