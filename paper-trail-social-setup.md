### 2. LinkedIn Company Page
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/company/create
**What you need:**
- Personal LinkedIn account (must have one first)
- Company email (same as above)
- Company details

**Steps:**

5. Website: https://papertrail.app
6. Industry: "Software Development" or "Financial Services"

### 3. GitHub Organization
**URL:** https://github.com/account/organizations/new
**What you need:**
- Personal GitHub account
- Organization name availability

**Steps:**
1. Click link → "Create a free organization"
2. Organization name: "papertrailapp" or "paper-trail"
3. Contact email: your email
4. Choose "My personal account" as owner
5. Select "Free" plan
6. Skip adding members for now
7. Upload organization avatar (logo)
8. Add description: "Open source expense tracking and receipt management"

---

### 4. Discord Server
**URL:** https://discord.com/channels/@me (then click "+" to create server)
**What you need:**
- Discord account (create at discord.com if needed)
- Just a few minutes

**Steps:**
1. Open Discord (web or app)
2. Click "+" next to your servers
3. Select "Create My Own"
4. Choose "For a club or community"
5. Server name: "Paper Trail"
6. Upload server icon (logo)
7. Set up channels:
   - #announcements
   - #general
   - #support
   - #feature-requests
   - #showcase
8. Create invite link (never expires)

---
